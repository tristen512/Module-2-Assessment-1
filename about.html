About Pine City Zoo

Pine City Zoo is a fun and interactive virtual zoo created as part of an IT Varsity student project. It allows visitors to explore different animals, learn facts about them, and enjoy a modern web experience with animations, dark mode, and background music.

This website demonstrates skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, including responsive design, animations, popups, and interactive features.

We hope you enjoy exploring the amazing world of animals in Pine City Zoo!
